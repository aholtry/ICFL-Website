4 dollars for an all day pass ( allows re-entry ) w/ stamp
3 dollars all day for students, seniors and military( allows re-entry ). ( with I.D )
*CASH ONLY at gate ( opens at 9am )

Kids 12 and under are free!

Rules

Rules regarding ICFL Gameday venues:

- NO OUTSIDE FOOD AND BEVERAGE. Centennial will have concessions onsite.
- Drugs, Alchohol and Smoking ( including vaping ) are prohibited onsite.
- changing in plain sight in parking lots etc. is prohibited ( please wear football pants to field at venues without locker rooms) families and children present.
- teams need to pickup the trash and any other debris on their sidelines at the completion of games. Also do not leave litter in adjacent areas/parking lots.
- no speeding or blaring music in parking lots.
- pets aren't allowed inside the facilities.
- no fighting, excessive profanity.
- exercise class and sportsmanship at all times.

In closing we are guests at all of these facilities and need to act as such, failure to respect these rules can/ will result in player discipline/ ejection aswell as spectators. We cannot afford for the actions of a few to be detrimental to the ICFL as a whole. Thanks!
